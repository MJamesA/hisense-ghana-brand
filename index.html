Section 05
Logo Usage

Protecting the wordmark keeps the brand strong. Respect the minimum clear space and size, and never alter the mark. When in doubt, reach for an approved master file.

Clear Space

Keep clear space around the wordmark at least equal to the cap-height of the “H”. Leave this margin free of type, imagery and other graphics.

Minimum Size

Digital · 120px wide min
Print · 25mm wide min

Do not reproduce the wordmark smaller than 25 mm / 120 px wide, to keep the italic letterforms legible.
