Section 05
Logo Usage

Protecting the logo keeps the brand strong. Respect the minimum clear space and size, and never alter the mark. When in doubt, reach for an approved master file.

Clear Space

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the icon mark on all four sides. Keep this margin free of type, imagery and competing graphics.

Minimum Size

Digital · 120px wide min
Icon · 24px min

Do not reproduce the full logo smaller than 25 mm / 120 px wide. Below that, use the standalone icon mark.
